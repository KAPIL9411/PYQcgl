Welcome to SSC CGL PYQ Practice ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our website.

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

We use browser local storage to save your practice progress, test scores, and preferences. This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience on our website. Cookies are small data files stored on your device.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

Ad Blocker Policy

Our website is provided free of charge and is supported by advertising revenue. To ensure we can continue providing this free service, we detect and restrict access to users who employ ad-blocking software or browsers with ad-blocking features enabled (such as Brave browser with Shields).

If you wish to use our service, you must:

  • Disable ad-blocking extensions or software for our website
  • Whitelist our website in your ad blocker settings
  • If using Brave browser, disable Shields for our website
